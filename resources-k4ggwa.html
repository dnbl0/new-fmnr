Knowledge for the Great Green Wall Action (K4GGWA) — resources, courses, webinars and connections supporting FMNR's role in the pan-African Great Green Wall initiative.

What is the Great Green Wall?

The Great Green Wall is Africa's flagship initiative to restore 100 million hectares of degraded land across the Sahel by 2030. FMNR is one of the most cost-effective and proven approaches to achieving this at scale.

The K4GGWA platform brings together evidence, training and networking to support FMNR practitioners and partners working within the Great Green Wall corridor.
