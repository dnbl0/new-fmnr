User Journey — Partners

How NGOs and implementation partners discover, evaluate and engage with FMNR to integrate it into their programmes.

Persona: Programme Manager at an NGO

Works for an international development organisation. Looking for scalable, evidence-based restoration approaches to include in upcoming project proposals. Needs proof of impact, implementation guides and a clear partnership pathway.

Evidence-driven Time-poor Needs credibility Partnership-focused

1. Awareness

Discovers FMNR through a conference, peer recommendation or web search

→ Homepage (hero, intro video)

Needs: Quick understanding of what FMNR is and its credibility

2. Understanding

Explores how FMNR works and what makes it different from other approaches

→ About FMNR, Tony Rinaudo page

Needs: Clear explanation of methodology and differentiation

3. Evidence Gathering

Reviews impact data, case studies and peer-reviewed research

→ Impact & Evidence, Interactive Map, Resources

Needs: Downloadable reports, statistics for proposals

4. Feasibility Assessment

Checks which regions FMNR has succeeded in, reviews training resources

→ Country Profiles, Training Guides

Needs: Context-specific information for their operating regions

5. Engagement

Explores partnership models, tiers and benefits

→ Partner With Us

Needs: Clear value proposition and commitment levels

6. Contact

Fills in partnership enquiry form or downloads an information pack

→ Contact Form, Newsletter Signup

Needs: Low-friction way to start the conversation
