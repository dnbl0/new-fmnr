User Journey — Funders

How donors and grant-makers assess FMNR's impact, review evidence and identify funding opportunities.

Persona: Programme Officer at a Foundation

Manages a climate/agriculture portfolio at a bilateral or private foundation. Evaluates dozens of initiatives annually. Needs rigorous data, clear cost-effectiveness metrics and confidence in scale potential before recommending funding.

Data-driven Comparative mindset Seeks ROI Needs scale evidence

1. Discovery

Encounters FMNR in a report, at a climate summit or via a referral

→ Homepage (credibility signals, scale numbers)

Needs: Immediate sense of scale and legitimacy

2. Due Diligence

Reviews hectares restored, beneficiaries reached, cost per hectare

→ Impact & Evidence (stats, charts, interactive map)

Needs: Hard numbers, peer-reviewed citations, methodology

3. Depth Research

Downloads research papers, reads case studies for specific regions

→ Resources (filtered by "Research"), Country Profiles

Needs: Downloadable PDFs, academic sources, geographic specificity

4. Alignment Check

Assesses how FMNR aligns with their portfolio themes (climate, food security, gender)

→ About FMNR, Partner page (impact areas)

Needs: Clear articulation of outcomes across multiple SDGs

5. Engagement

Reaches out to discuss funding opportunities or co-investment

→ Partner With Us, Contact

Needs: Senior contact, clear ask, proposal-ready content
