User Journey — Adopters

How farmers and communities learn about, adopt and practise FMNR techniques to restore their land.

Persona: Smallholder Farmer

Manages a small farm in a degraded landscape. Has heard about FMNR from a neighbour or community meeting. Needs simple, visual instructions and proof that FMNR will improve yields. May access the site via a shared phone with limited data.

Visual learner Mobile-first Low bandwidth Wants practical steps

1. Curiosity

Hears about FMNR from a neighbour, community leader or field worker

→ Homepage (video hero — visual, immediate)

Needs: Instant visual understanding, no jargon

2. Inspiration

Sees success stories from farmers in similar regions

→ Impact page, Country Profiles (interactive map)

Needs: Relatable stories, before/after imagery

3. Learning

Watches how-to videos and reads step-by-step guides

→ Resources (filtered by "Training"), About FMNR

Needs: Simple visual guides, video content, downloadable for offline

4. Practice

Starts applying techniques on a small section of their farm

→ Training Guides (PDF downloads)

Needs: Printable reference cards, troubleshooting tips

5. Community

Connects with local FMNR groups or shares results with neighbours

→ Contact, Newsletter

Needs: Local contact information, community links
