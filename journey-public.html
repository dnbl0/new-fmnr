User Journey — General Public

How curious visitors learn what FMNR is, explore inspiring stories and share content with their networks.

Persona: Environmentally Curious Individual

Saw a social media post or news article about reforestation. Wants to understand FMNR quickly and feel inspired. May share content or sign up for a newsletter but is unlikely to have a professional stake.

Casual browser Story-driven Social sharer Short attention span

1. Arrival

Clicks a link from social media, news article or search result

→ Homepage (video hero — emotional, visual hook)

Needs: Immediate emotional connection, visual storytelling

2. Exploration

Scrolls through the homepage, watches the video, scans key stats

→ Homepage (impact numbers, scroll animations)

Needs: Bite-sized information, compelling visuals

3. Deeper Interest

Clicks into a story about Tony Rinaudo or a specific country

→ Tony Rinaudo, Interactive Map

Needs: Human stories, narrative arc, shareable moments

4. Sharing

Shares a stat, video clip or article with their network

→ Any page (social sharing metadata, OG images)

Needs: Quotable stats, shareable visuals, good meta tags

5. Staying Connected

Signs up for the newsletter to follow progress

→ Newsletter signup (footer or contact page)

Needs: Simple one-field signup, clear value proposition
